Motivation: 旨在通过恒星冷却的天体物理观测，探索和约束轻自旋2粒子作为暗物质的可能性。

Method: 使用双度规理论计算在恒星内部通过光生过程和轫致辐射产生自旋2粒子的速率，并考虑等离子体屏蔽效应。将计算出的能量损失率与观测到的恒星冷却极限进行比较，从而推导对粒子质量和耦合强度的限制。

Result: 研究结果在5-30 eV的质量范围内排除了大片参数空间，得出的限制与现有的天体物理和宇宙学限制相当。

Conclusion: 如果自旋2粒子是宇宙的暗物质，那么这项工作利用恒星冷却数据为其参数空间提供了强有力的天体物理约束。

Abstract: Using the bimetric formalism, we compute the production and emission rates of
light spin-2 particles in non-degenerate stellar interiors through
photoproduction and bremsstrahlung processes, including the effects of plasma
screening. By comparing the resulting energy-loss rates with observational
limits on stellar cooling, we derive bounds on the coupling strength and mass
of the spin-2 particle. Assuming these particles are the dark matter of the
Universe, the obtained constraints are competitive with existing astrophysical
and cosmological limits, excluding a wide region of parameter space in the mass
range 5-30 eV.
