When these emergent phases are approached, QFI diverges across multiple wave vectors in momentum space. Taken together, QFI is not only helpful as a lower bound for entanglement depth, but serves as a momentum-resolved probe of entanglement that offers a novel perspective on quantum critical phenomena.
